From 2021 to 2024, I have accumulated over 930 hours of volunteer service through activities such as organizing educational outreach events for rural elementary schools, assisting in mental health facilities, and supporting nonprofit operations at Teach for Taiwan (TFT).

During my time with TFT, I served as an Organizational Operations Assistant, where I helped plan, execute, and improve internal logistics and data systems. My contributions included managing mailing logistics, workspace organization, device tracking, and the development of tools to automate workflows.

A major part of my work involved using Google Apps Script to build internal systems such as:

  • A volunteer book borrowing and return tracking system
  • A spreadsheet-powered training record generator for Google Docs and Calendar
  • A resource application management tool to streamline backend coordination

These experiences sharpened my technical skills while teaching me how to collaborate in mission-driven environments. I learned to communicate across departments, understand stakeholder needs, and iterate on feedback to improve tools for real-world use.

Beyond office automation, I also co-led service-learning initiatives with Spring Radiance Club, coordinating over 50 students to design and run educational programs for children in rural areas and hospitals. This hands-on experience deepened my understanding of social responsibility and team leadership.
